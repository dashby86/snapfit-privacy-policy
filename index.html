üíæ Data Storage

Your workout data is stored locally on your device using:

SnapFit only requests permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Screen wake lock - to keep screen on during fullscreen timer mode

No network permissions - the app cannot access the internet.

üåê Third-Party Services

External APIs: None used

üöÄ How SnapFit Works

SnapFit is a completely offline workout timer app that:

  • Creates and manages workout timers locally on your device
  • Stores your custom workouts in local device storage
  • Plays audio alerts and provides haptic feedback during workouts
  • Offers fullscreen timer mode with Chromecast support (local network only)
